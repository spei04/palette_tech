Stage 1

Ingestion & Understanding

The agent begins by ingesting financial documents from configured sources. It does not rely on structured data — it reads and interprets documents the same way a human would.

mail

Email ingestion

Invoices forwarded or CC'd to a dedicated inbox are automatically picked up, parsed, and queued for processing. Attachments in PDF, CSV, or image format are supported.

upload_file

File upload

Invoices can be submitted directly via the Palette dashboard or pushed via API from your existing ERP or document management system.

description

Contract parsing

Master service agreements, purchase orders, and vendor contracts are ingested once and stored as the source of truth. The agent references these when evaluating every invoice from that vendor.

ingestion log

02

Stage 2

Verification

Each invoice is systematically checked against its corresponding contract. The agent does not approve anything it cannot verify against a known source of truth.

price_check

Pricing & rate validation

Line-item unit prices are compared against contract rates. Surcharges are validated against agreed-upon caps or indexed market rates.

straighten

Quantity matching

Quantities billed are compared against purchase orders or delivery confirmations where available.

content_copy

Duplicate detection

Invoice numbers, amounts, and dates are checked against payment history to catch resubmissions across billing cycles.

schedule

Payment terms check

Invoice due dates and early-payment discounts are verified against the payment terms defined in the contract.

03

Stage 3

Decision-Making

After verification, the agent produces a decision with a confidence score and full reasoning trace. The outcome is one of three states.

check_circle

Approved

All checks passed within configured thresholds. The invoice is cleared for payment execution. No human action required.

flag

Flagged for review

A discrepancy was found but is within a range that may be explainable (e.g. an approved change order not yet in the system). A human reviewer receives the invoice, the discrepancy detail, and the agent's reasoning.

cancel

Rejected

A definitive violation was detected — duplicate invoice, pricing that exceeds contract maximums, or other clear breach of terms. The invoice is held and the vendor is notified.

Confidence scoring

Every decision includes a confidence score (0–100%) derived from the quality of the contract data, the number of checks that passed or failed, and the similarity of the invoice to previously processed invoices from the same vendor. Scores below your configured threshold automatically route to human review regardless of outcome.

04

Stage 4

Execution

Approved invoices are handed off to the payment execution layer. This stage is only available on Enterprise plans and requires a connected banking API integration.

payments

Payment initiation

Payments are initiated via your connected banking API according to the invoice's payment terms. Early payment discounts are automatically applied when advantageous.

sync_alt

Real-time reconciliation

Payment confirmations are logged and pushed back to your ERP or accounting system, keeping your books up to date without manual entry.

history_edu

Immutable audit log

Every action — ingestion, verification check, decision, payment — is written to an append-only log with timestamps, agent reasoning, and the data inputs used at decision time.

Security & Compliance

Built for Financial Data

lock

VPC deployment (Enterprise)

Your invoice and contract data never touches shared infrastructure. Enterprise deployments run entirely within your own VPC.

verified_user

SOC 2 Type II (in progress)

We are actively pursuing SOC 2 Type II certification. Current security posture documentation is available under NDA for enterprise evaluations.

key

Encryption at rest & in transit

All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Access is controlled via role-based permissions.

history_edu

Immutable audit logs

Every agent action is written to an append-only log. Logs are exportable for internal audit and regulatory review.
