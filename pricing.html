FAQ

Common Questions

Why don't you publish fixed prices? add

We're in private beta. The right price depends heavily on your invoice volume, existing systems, and how deep the integration needs to go. Publishing a fixed number before we understand your environment would serve us, not you. We'd rather have a short conversation and give you a number that actually makes sense.

What does the onboarding process look like? add

We start by ingesting a sample of your existing invoices and contracts so the system can learn your vendor relationships and contract structures. Early customers work closely with our team throughout this process. We do not expect you to configure the system yourself — that's our job.

Is our financial data secure? add

Security is a first-class requirement, not an add-on. Enterprise deployments run within your own VPC — your invoice and contract data never touches shared infrastructure. We are building toward SOC 2 Type II certification and can share our current security posture in detail during a technical conversation.

Can a human still review decisions before payment? add

Yes, and for most early customers we recommend starting there. You configure approval thresholds — invoices under a certain amount from known vendors with high confidence scores are auto-approved, while anything outside those bounds goes to a human review queue. You decide how much autonomy the system has, and can expand it over time as trust builds.

What integrations do you support? add

We are actively building integrations with major ERP systems (SAP, NetSuite, Oracle), accounting platforms (QuickBooks, Xero), and banking APIs. For early customers, we scope integrations as part of the onboarding conversation. If you use a system that isn't listed, tell us — it helps us prioritize our roadmap.
